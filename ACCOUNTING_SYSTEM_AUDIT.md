## Executive Summary

This report lists missing components and enhancements highlighted during the external audit on February 5, 2026. Items are grouped by module to guide implementation and prioritization.

---

## 1. Payment Voucher --- done implemented and reviewed

- View option for detailed voucher information.
- Delete option to remove erroneous or lingering vouchers.
- Edit option to correct voucher errors.
- Print option to produce audit copies.
- Period selector for monthly, quarterly, and yearly reporting.

---

## 2. Sales Ledger -  --- done implemented and reviewed

- View option for detailed sale information.
- Edit option for correcting errors.
- Print option to produce audit copies.
- Aging analysis for outstanding receivables by time bucket.
- Estimates and quotations for pre-sale cost planning and price confirmation.
- Sales order to confirm approved sales before service delivery.
- Till sale for instant point-of-sale recognition.
- Product area code to track sales by category or department.
- Period selector for monthly, quarterly, and yearly reporting.
✅ 1. Invoice (Sales Voucher)
Purpose:
Used after a sale has happened and you are billing the customer.
Contents typically include:
- Customer details
- Invoice number
- Invoice date & due date
- Products/services sold
- Quantity & rate
- Discounts (if any)
- Taxes (VAT/GST)
- Total amount
- Payment status (paid / unpaid / part-paid)
- Payment method (cash, bank, mobile money)
✔️ Your list is correct.

✅ 2. Estimate (Proforma Invoice / Estimate)
Purpose:
Given before the sale, to show expected cost.
Contents:
- Estimated products/services
- Approximate quantities
- Estimated prices
- Estimated taxes
- Validity period
✔️ Correct.
In Tally, this is usually done using a Proforma Invoice or Optional Voucher.

✅ 3. Quotation
Purpose:
A formal price offer with fixed terms.
Contents:
- Product/service description
- Fixed prices
- Quantities
- Tax details
- Validity date (e.g., 30 days)
- Terms & conditions
✔️ Correct.
In Tally, quotations are also created using Optional Vouchers.

✅ 4. Sales Order
Purpose:
Customer has agreed to buy, but delivery or invoicing is pending.
Contents:
- Customer details
- Ordered items
- Quantities ordered
- Expected delivery date
- Order status (pending, delivered, cancelled)
✔️ Correct.
In Tally, this is a Sales Order Voucher.

✅ 5. Till Sale / Cash Sale / POS Sale
Purpose:
Immediate sale with instant payment, usually for walk‑in customers.
Contents:
- Walk‑in customer (often “Cash Customer”)
- Items sold
- Cash / mobile money / card payment
- Receipt number
- Daily sales summary
